CO2 and Unemployment from 2005 to 2017

The figure shown above plots the percentage of Unemployment in the US, alongside Carbon Monoxide captured in the atmosphere. We can see that the release of CO has been decreasing steadily over time, but during the peak of Unemployment (2009), it decreases at a much faster rate.
