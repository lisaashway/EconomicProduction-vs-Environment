O3 and Unemployment from 2005 to 2017

The figure shown above plots the percentage of Unemployment in the US, alongside Ozone (O3) captured in the atmosphere. While Ozone naturally occurs in the upper atmosphere, it is highly dangerous when closer to the ground. It is the only pollutant analyzed that steadily increases over time - however in 2009 it takes a sharp dive as an economic recession begins.
